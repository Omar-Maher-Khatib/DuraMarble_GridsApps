About DuraMarble

Crafting Excellence Since 1995

Our Story

DuraMarble has been a leading provider of premium marble and stone solutions for over three decades. Founded in 1995, we began as a small family business with a passion for bringing the finest natural stone to homes and businesses.

Today, we've grown into a trusted name in the industry, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the country. Our commitment to quality, craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction remains unwavering.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional marble and stone products that transform spaces into timeless works of art, while delivering unparalleled customer service and sustainable practices.
